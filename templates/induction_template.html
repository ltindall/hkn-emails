Congratulations! On behalf of the members of IEEE-HKN, Kappa Psi Chapter, it is my pleasure to invite you to join IEEE-Eta Kappa Nu (IEEE-HKN), the Engineering Honor Society.

HKN uniquely honors ECE, CSE, MAE, and NANO students who have achieved substantial success academically, possess leadership qualities, and have noteworthy character. Your success at the University of California, San Diego qualifies you for admission into IEEE-HKN.

As an IEEE-HKN member, you will have the opportunity to work with other IEEE-HKN members and faculty in service programs that will refine your skills for your professional future. You will develop leadership abilities and have an opportunity to cultivate a lasting network between you, your peers, and your faculty.

Companies recognize the importance of being an IEEE-HKN member and the professional skills that members can contribute. Often, membership in this honor society is an influential factor in their decision to make a professional job offer.
