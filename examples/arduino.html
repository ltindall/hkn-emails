Hello Students,

HKN is proud to host Part 1 of the Build Your Own Arduino Workshop Series! This is a 4 part workshop series where you will learn how to build your own functioning Arduino from its bare components.

Part 1 will be constructing an Arduino on a breadboard. Future workshops cover creating a Printed Circuit Board (PCB) for the Arduino using EagleCAD (Part 2), then soldering through-hole and surface-mount components onto the PCB (Part 3), and finally programming your newly made Arduino (Part 4).

This workshop series is designed for you to broaden you understanding of Arduino schematics, PCB layout, soldering skills, and programming.

Spaces are limited due to supplies!
